ARCINDA

Highlight

Bali Art Festival Performance

During the summer of 2024, Arcinda was invited to perform at the Bali Art Festival in Indonesia for the second time. We have collaborated with local groups and universities to bring together a performance that tells the story of Arjuna and Srikandi. We are thankful for the invitation, and hope to continue promoting culture to Colorado, Bali, Indonesia and beyond!

Who we are

ARCINDA (The Arts and Culture of Indonesia) is a non-profit organization, with 501 (c)(3) status. Through gamelan music and dance, our mission is to promote traditional Indonesian culture all across Colorado and the USA. Our influence has allowed us to receive several grants from City of Fort Collins, City of Longmont, and other organizations.

Join us!

Anyone who is interested in learning more about Indonesian culture is free to join ARCINDA for free! Feel free to contact us, either by sending us a message or by connecting with us on performance/rehearsal days!

Our routine consists of rehearsals at volunteering members' residences with both practice for gamelan and dance. Our rehearsals will prepare us for our upcoming performances. Food and drinks are provided!
