Organization

Who we are

ARCINDA (The Arts and Culture of Indonesia) is a non-profit organization, with 501 (c)(3) status. Through gamelan music and dance, our mission is to promote traditional Indonesian culture all across Colorado and the USA. Our influence has allowed us to receive several grants from City of Fort Collins, City of Longmont, and other organizations.

We are active in various cities in Colorado, with our main practice areas being in Loveland and Denver. We hold yearly performances at Denver Art Museum, Dragon Boat Festival, CU Boulder International, and also frequently perform at various locations/events in Colorado. Not only limited to Colorado and the USA, we have participated in the Bali Arts Festival in Indonesia with both American and local Bali communities!

What we do

We provide a multitude of activities that aim to sustain our cultural impact, such as:

  • Indonesian gamelan and dance performances
  • Event arrangements, presentations, workshops, and education
  • Group rehearsals and workshops for gamelan introduction/practice
  • Collaboration with other performance groups
  • Invite members who are interested in Indonesian culture for free

Purposes

Our organization aims to fulfill the following purposes:

  • To promote the Arts of Indonesia in specific, and Asia in general, to the USA and other countries
  • To anticipate the impact of globalization by increasing good impact to it's maximum
  • To build a strong relationship and deep understanding of a multi-cultural world
